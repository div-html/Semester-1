Your dream getaway in the heart of Darjeeling. Set against the breathtaking backdrop of the Himalayas and nestled among vibrant tea gardens, our hotel offers a serene escape from the chaos of everyday life. Whether you're visiting for leisure, a romantic retreat, or a peaceful workation, Paradise Hotel promises an unforgettable
Located just a short walk from the iconic Mall Road, our guests enjoy the convenience of exploring local attractions while returning to a peaceful oasis. The hotel offers stunning views of the snow-capped Kanchenjunga, especially magical during sunrise and sunset, making it a photographer's paradise.
Our beautifully furnished rooms combine the charm of colonial architecture with modern amenities such as high-speed Wi-Fi, 24/7 room service, and heated blankets to ensure your comfort in the cool mountain air. From deluxe suites with private balconies to cozy family rooms, we have something for everyone.
Indulge in authentic North-Eastern and multi-cuisine dishes at our in-house restaurant, or enjoy a cup of freshly brewed Darjeeling tea at our garden cafe. You can also unwind with a rejuvenating spa session, take a nature walk, or simply sit by the fire and enjoy the silence of the hills.
Our courteous and friendly staff are always ready to assist you - from planning day tours to arranging transport or customizing your stay for special occasions. Cleanliness, safety, and guest satisfaction are at the core of everything we do.
At Paradise Hotel, we don't just offer rooms - we create experiences filled with warmth, tradition, and tranquility. Come, discover the charm of the Queen of the Hills with us. Your perfect mountain escape awaits!
